Project Overview

This project showcases a systematic approach to mobile app logo design, beginning with hand-drawn sketches and evolving into polished digital concepts. The collection demonstrates versatility across different app categories while maintaining consistent design principles.

Design Process

  • Initial concept exploration through hand-drawn sketches
  • Refinement and systematic categorization of ideas
  • Digital translation with monochrome variations
  • Color palette development and final execution

Key Features

Each logo in the collection demonstrates strong typography, memorable iconography, and scalable design principles. The hand-drawn foundation ensures organic, human-centered aesthetics while the digital execution provides professional polish.

Technical Approach

The project balances creative exploration with systematic methodology, ensuring each design is both visually striking and functionally appropriate for mobile app branding. The progression from sketches to final designs shows clear evolution and refinement.
